Help & Support

Here you’ll find answers to common questions and guidance on how to use our Task Management System.

How do I add a new task?

Click on the Add New Task button from the dashboard. Fill in the task details and press save. The task will appear in your task list.

How can I mark a task as completed?

Open the Tasks page, then select the checkbox beside the task you want to mark as completed. The system will automatically update it.

Why can’t I see my tasks?

Make sure you are logged in. If you still can’t see your tasks, refresh the page or try logging out and back in again.

How do I delete a task?

On the Tasks page, click the delete button next to the task. A confirmation popup will appear before it is removed permanently.

Who can I contact for help?

If you experience technical issues, please email our support team at support@example.com.

Where can I learn more?

Check the About Us page to learn more about our team and project. Updates will also be posted there.
